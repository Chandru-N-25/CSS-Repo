bird

Owls are birds from the order Strigiformes, which includes over
200 species of mostly solitary and nocturnal birds of prey typified by
an upright stance, a large, broad head, binocular vision, binaural hearing,
sharp talons, and feathers adapted for silent flight.

lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India.
It has a muscular, broad-chested body; short, rounded head; round ears; and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
It is sexually dimorphic; adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane

tiger

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera.
It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside.
An apex predator, it primarily preys on ungulates, such as deer and wild boar.
